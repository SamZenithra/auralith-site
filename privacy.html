Privacy Policy

How Auralith collects, uses and protects your personal data when you use Zenithra.

🗓 Last updated: March 2026 · Auralith, United Kingdom
Section 01

Who We Are

Auralith ("we", "our", "us") is a digital wellness company based in the United Kingdom, operating the website auralith.uk and the Zenithra web application at app.auralith.uk.

We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Section 02

Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Account Data: name and email address when you register for Zenithra
  • Health & Wellness Data: fasting logs, weight entries, task records, mood logs, habit data and activity data you voluntarily enter into the app — stored securely in our cloud database
  • Usage Data: pages visited, time spent, device type and browser information collected automatically
  • Communications: email address when contacting us or signing up for notifications
  • Payment Data: payment is processed securely by Stripe — we never store your card details on our servers
Section 03

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalise the Zenithra app experience
  • To calculate your Life Score, calorie targets and health insights
  • To send trial reminder emails and important account notifications
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To improve our services and fix technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations
Section 04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider with enterprise-grade security. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

🔒 We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your health and wellness data is private and used only to power your personal Zenithra experience.
Section 05

Third-Party Services

Zenithra uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — secure cloud database and authentication
  • Stripe — payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Netlify — app hosting and delivery
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

Each provider has their own privacy policy and data protection commitments.

Section 06

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting prior processing
Section 07

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription and request account deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days.

Some data may be retained longer where required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as fraud prevention.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

Zenithra is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

Section 09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or via an in-app notification. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
